FAQs

Q: What's an affiliate program?

A: An affiliate or referral program is a type of performance-based marketing. A business rewards its affiliate partners for sending customers to its site by using their own marketing efforts. The GoAnimate affiliate program rewards affiliates for business or school account signups.

Q: Can anyone be an affiliate?

A: We evaluate all affiliates on a case by case basis. We are looking for working websites, with relevant content and quality traffic. We will actively avoid certain content types (ex: erotic, gambling) and anything too spammy.

Q: How does the referral work?

A: Once your account is approved, you will gain access to our affiliate management interface. Visit http://affiliates.goanimate.com and log in. From there, you can obtain the affiliate links and creative for use on your site. If you need help, we have a wizard which can walk you through the process.

Q: How do affiliates get paid?

A: You will earn a commission on every qualified new subscription (excluding renewals) made by visitors you have referred. The Offer details specified in the affiliate management interface will define the qualified subscriptions. Most Offers will be for GoAnimate for Business. Later, some will be for GoAnimate for Schools. There are no Offers for GoPlus. Earned commissions will be credited to your account 60 days after the transaction (that delay is necessary to protect us both from the effects of chargebacks and refunds). Payment will be transferred to your PayPal account at the end of each month, provided the balance due is over US$500. If the balance due is less than that, the credit will roll over to the next month.
If you have any further questions, do not hesitate to let us know. Email us at affiliates@goanimate.com
